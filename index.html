Traditional Japanese Haiku
日本の伝統的な俳句
Japanese Haiku is a traditional form of poetry with a strict structure of three lines, consisting of 5, 7, and 5 syllables, respectively. It captures the essence of nature, evoking emotions and contemplation in a concise and profound manner.
日本の俳句は伝統的な詩形で、厳格な三行で構成され、それぞれが5、7、5の音節で表現されます。自然の本質を捉え、感情や思索を簡潔かつ深遠に表現します。
野に咲く 赤い花や 風の音
Blooming in fields, Red flowers sway gently, Whispering breeze
